About Saints Row: Gat Out of Hell Modding

Saints Row: Gat Out of Hell is a standalone expansion with its own modding ecosystem. While the toolset is smaller than SR3 and SR4, the community has adapted existing tools and created GOOH-specific utilities. The game is perfect for modders looking to customize gameplay and visuals in a focused environment.

For tools and community support, visit the Saints Row Mods community where you can find GOOH-specific resources and connect with other modders.
