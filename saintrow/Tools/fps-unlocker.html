What is the FPS Unlocker?

The FPS Unlocker is a simple yet essential utility that removes the default frame rate cap from the GOG version of Saints Row 2. By default, Saints Row 2 on PC is locked to 30 FPS, which feels sluggish on modern high-refresh-rate monitors. This tool unlocks the frame rate, allowing the game to run at your monitor's native refresh rate — 60, 120, 144 Hz, or higher.

With the FPS Unlocker, gameplay becomes significantly smoother, camera movement feels more responsive, and the overall experience is transformed. It's a must-have for anyone playing Saints Row 2 on modern hardware.

⚡ Performance Impact

Unlocking the frame rate does not negatively affect game stability. The game engine handles higher frame rates well, and the unlocker simply removes the artificial cap.

Troubleshooting

Game won't launch after applying

Fix: Restore the original .exe from backup. Ensure you're using the GOG version.

FPS still capped at 30

Fix: Verify the unlocker was applied correctly. Check if VSync is forcing a cap in your GPU settings.

Screen tearing issues

Fix: Enable VSync through your graphics card control panel (NVIDIA Control Panel or AMD Adrenalin).

Game feels too fast

Fix: Some game logic may be tied to frame rate. Cap FPS to 60 using your GPU control panel if needed.
