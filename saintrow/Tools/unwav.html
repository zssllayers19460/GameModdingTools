What is UnWAV?

UnWAV is a specialized audio tool designed for Saints Row 2 modding that handles the conversion and repacking of audio files back into game-compatible formats. Troubleshooting

Audio doesn't play in-game after repacking

Fix: Verify your WAV files match the original format specifications (sample rate, bit depth, channels). Use a reference file to check correct settings.

Game crashes when audio should play

Fix: Ensure your edited audio files aren't corrupted. Test with a single replacement before batch processing.

Audio sounds distorted or sped up

Fix: Sample rate mismatch — ensure your edited files use the same sample rate as the original (usually 22,050 Hz or 44,100 Hz).

UnWAV won't process my files

Fix: Make sure your WAV files are in PCM format (not compressed). Convert using Audacity if needed.

Pro Tips for Audio Modding

🎧 Always Use Reference Files

Keep original extracted files as references for format specifications. Compare your edits to ensure proper settings.

📊 Normalize Volume Levels

Ensure your custom audio matches the game's volume levels. Use normalization to prevent audio from being too loud or too quiet.

🔁 Test Incrementally

Replace one audio file at a time and test. This makes it easier to identify problematic files if something goes wrong.

💾 Backup Originals

Always keep backups of original XWB files. You'll need them if something goes wrong with your mod.
