About Original Saints Row Modding

The original Saints Row (2006) has a smaller but dedicated modding community compared to later titles. Most modding focuses on texture modifications and simple model swaps. The game's engine and file structure are simpler than later games, making it a good starting point for beginners interested in Saints Row modding.

Note: The original game was released on Xbox 360 and later on PC. PC modding tools are more readily available. For the best modding experience and community support, consider checking out Saints Row Mods for additional resources and tutorials.
