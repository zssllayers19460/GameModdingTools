About GTA Modding

The Grand Theft Auto series has one of the largest and most active modding communities in gaming history. From simple texture replacements to total conversion mods, the tools available for GTA games are incredibly powerful. Whether you're looking to create custom vehicles, script new missions, or completely transform Los Santos, Liberty City, or San Andreas, the right tools are here.

Getting Started: New to GTA modding? Start with OpenIV for GTA V/IV or IMG Tool for San Andreas. Learn the basics of archive structure before diving into advanced modding. For the latest tools and community support, visit the GTA5-Mods community.
